# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** May 4, 2026

**Stemax Development** (represented by **Max Stempleuski**) operates the **TorMemoryCards** mobile application (the "Service"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service.

## 1. Information Collection and Use
We built TorMemoryCards as a Free app. This Service is provided at no cost and is intended for use as is.

**We do not collect or store any personally identifiable information (PII).** However, the app uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify your device for technical and advertising purposes.

## 2. Children's Privacy and COPPA Compliance
This Application is designed for children (category 3+) and strictly complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and Google Play Families Policy.

* We **do not** knowingly collect personal data from children.
* We **do not** use behavioral (personalized) advertising.
* We have configured our advertising providers to serve only context-aware, age-appropriate ads.
* All game progress data stays locally on your device.

## 3. Third-Party Services and Advertising
To support the service and provide technical stability, we use the following third-party providers:

* **Google Play Services:** Used for app functionality and stability.
* **Google AdMob:** Used to show rewarded video ads. We have configured AdMob to:
    * Tag ad requests as child-directed (COPPA).
    * Limit data collection to non-identifying technical information.
    * Use a "G" content rating for all advertisements.

You can find their policies here:
* [Google Play Services Privacy Policy](https://google.com)
* [Google AdMob Privacy Policy](https://google.com)

## 4. Data Security
Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no risk of your personal information being compromised. Any technical data processed by third-party SDKs is handled over encrypted protocols.
