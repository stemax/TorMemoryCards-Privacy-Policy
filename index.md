# Privacy Policy
**Last Updated:** February 28, 2026

**Stemax Development** (represented by **Max Stempleuski**) operates the **TorMemoryCards** mobile application (the "Service"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service.

## 1. Information Collection and Use
We built TorMemoryCards as a Free app. To support the continued development of this Service, we use third-party advertising.

While we do not collect personal information like names or emails ourselves, our third-party partners may collect and use your **device's advertising identifier** (e.g., Google Advertising ID) to serve advertisements within the app.

* We do not ask for names, email addresses, or physical locations.
* The app does not access your device's contacts, camera, or files.

## 2. Children's Privacy
This Application is designed for all ages, including children, and complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). 

* We **do not** knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.
* Advertising in the app is configured to show content appropriate for families.
* We do not use behavioral tracking for users identified as children.
* All game progress data stays locally on your device.

## 3. Third-Party Services
The app uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify your device for technical stability and advertising purposes:

* [Google Play Services Privacy Policy](https://google.com)
* [Yandex Mobile Ads Privacy Policy](https://yandex.com)

## 4. Data Security
The transmission of technical data (such as advertising IDs) by our third-party partners is performed over encrypted connections (HTTPS) to ensure industry-standard security.
